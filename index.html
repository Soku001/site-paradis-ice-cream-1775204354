About Paradis Ice Cream

Located at 700 S Spring St, Los Angeles, CA, Paradis Ice Cream is a beloved destination for handcrafted, premium ice cream. Our philosophy is simple: use the finest ingredients to create unforgettable flavors that bring joy to every customer.

Our dedicated Expert Team combines culinary artistry with a passion for perfection, ensuring Quality Guaranteed in every scoop. From classic favorites to innovative seasonal specials, we invite you to taste the difference. We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming atmosphere where every visit is a treat.
